This clever halter is adjustable in 5 different ways... 2 buckles on the crown, 2 buckles on the noseband, and 1 buckle connecting to the nose piece under the chin. Rust-proof solid brass hardware for added style and durability.

  • Double layered leather by Wickett & Craig of America
  • 1" wide leather (weanling and yearling sizes are 3/4" wide)
  • Adjustable in 5 different ways
  • 2 buckles on the crown; 2 buckles on the noseband; 1 buckle connecting to the nose piece under the chin
  • Rust-proof solid brass hardware
